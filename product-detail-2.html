Description

Details

This premium quality hoodie features the official university logo embroidered on the front. Made from a soft, comfortable cotton blend fabric that's perfect for everyday wear.

The hoodie includes a spacious kangaroo pocket, adjustable drawstring hood, and ribbed cuffs and hem for a comfortable fit. Available in multiple sizes to ensure the perfect fit for everyone.

Whether you're heading to class, cheering at a game, or just relaxing on campus, this hoodie is the perfect way to show your university pride.
